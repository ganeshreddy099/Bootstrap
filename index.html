Welcome to Bugatti Automobiles

Automobiles Ettore Bugatti was a German then French manufacturer
of high-performance automobiles. The company was founded in 1909
in the then-German city of Molsheim, Alsace, by the Italian-born industrial
designer Ettore Bugatti. The cars were known for their design beauty and their
many race victories. Famous Bugatti automobiles include the Type 35 Grand Prix cars,
the Type 41 "Royale", the Type 57 "Atlantic" and the Type 55 sports car.
